Privacy Policy

QR Scanner Pro — Last updated: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how QR Scanner Pro ("the App", "we") handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Scan history — codes you scan are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload your scan contents to any server. Deleting the app (or clearing history) removes them permanently.
  • Email address (optional) — only if you voluntarily subscribe to updates during onboarding. You can skip this step entirely.
  • Usage and device data — anonymous analytics (app opens, feature usage, device model, OS version) via Google Firebase to improve the App.

2. Camera Permission

The App uses your camera exclusively to scan QR codes and barcodes in real time. No photos or video are recorded, stored, or transmitted.

3. Advertising

The free version shows ads served by third-party networks:

  • Google AdMob
  • Yandex Ads

These networks may use device identifiers to show personalized ads, subject to your consent. Purchasing Premium removes all ads.

4. In-App Purchases

Payments are processed by Samsung Galaxy Store (Samsung Checkout). We never see or store your payment details.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with the service providers listed above (ads, analytics) as required to operate the App.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data (e.g., a subscribed email) at any time by contacting us. You can withdraw ad-personalization consent in your device settings.

7. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; changes will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
